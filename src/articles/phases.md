# Phases

1. **Assignment Phase**

   - Players assign their leaders to missions.

2. **Command Phase**

   - Activate a System
   - Reveal a Mission

3. **Refresh Phase**

   1. Retrieve Leaders

   2. Draw Missions, discard down to 10 cards

   3. Launch Two Probe Droids

   4. Draw Objective

   5. Advance Time Marker

      1. Recruit Leader
      2. Build Units

   6. Deploy Units
